About Us

Sound Revival is proud to be the first and only brick and mortar studio in Columbia, TN. By offering not only state of the art equipment and experienced producers, but cd pressings as well as screen printing; Sound Revival Studios is your one stop shop for starting or continuing your music career. Sound Revival Studios has all of your recording needs. We are located directly across the street from the Columbia State University Campus.
